MISSION

Society for Out of Frame Education (in short, “EVO”) has a mission to educate all age groups in the critical way of thinking and to promote freedom of thought, opinion and expression. Members of EVO are gathered around the joint experience of doing scientific research and education of youth. While aspiring to their own professional excellence, they advocate responsibility towards the entire community and work as volunteers to help overcome existent challenges within the society. EVO's aim is to create a network of experts in Croatia and abroad that are interested to actively counteract negative societal trends, especially in education, through synergy of current and development of new projects. EVO's current projects are directed towards improvement of scientific literacy, communication and culture.
