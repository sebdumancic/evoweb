International Helmholtz Research School of Biophysics and Soft Matter


The IHRS BioSoft offers a guest student programme for Master students in biology, chemistry, and physics and related subjects. The guest students will mainly work on a project with one of our PhD students or with a more advanced scientist from July 27 to October 2, 2014. In addition, they will participate in an accompanying course program that includes lab courses and seminars in other IHRS BioSoft groups.

 

 

 

12th Greta Pifat-Mrzljak International School of Biophysics


The School is focused on Biomolecular Complexes and Assemblies and takes place in Primošten, Croatia. Through the years, the School has been attended by more than one thousand Ph.D. students and postdocs interested in becoming acquainted with the state-of-the-art in biophysics. The lecturers have always been of the highest quality, including top scientists in their respective disciplines and several Nobel laureates.
