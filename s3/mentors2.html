Mentors


The mediocre teacher tells. The good teacher explains. The superior teacher demonstrates. The great teacher inspires. --William Arthur Ward
Summer School of Science wouldn't exist it weren't for the immense enthusiasm of its mentors, university students and young scientists who come to share their passion for science with our participants. Whether as group leaders, working closely on daily basis with their students, or lecturers, giving passionate and motivating talks about their latest research, our mentors serve as role models and inspire young students to learn more and achieve their best.

We would like to express our deepest gratitude to all mentors for being a part of Summer School of Science.
